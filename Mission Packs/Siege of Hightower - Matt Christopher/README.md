# Siege of Hightower Mission Pack

By Matt Christopher

A 20-mission narrative pack for Warhammer: The Horus Heresy (Age of Darkness), covering the Loyalist-versus-Traitor fight across the five warzones of Hightower on Gabriel's Gate: the Urban District, Industrial Zone, Administratum, Balefire Fortress, and Axios Star Port. Each mission sets its own objectives, deployment map, game length, and bespoke special rule, with several campaign-flagged outcomes ("advise an EO") for organised play.

## Contents

- Category: mission pack
- Missions: 20 (including 4 Zone Mortalis missions)
- Warzones: Urban District (4), Industrial Zone (4), Administratum (4), Balefire Fortress (5), Axios Star Port (3)
- Play style: narrative campaign, siege, urban
- Points range: any

## Compatibility

- Replaces: nothing
- Requires: nothing
- Self-contained. Zone Mortalis missions expect the standard Age of Darkness Zone Mortalis rules; all missions reference the core Age of Darkness Rulebook (Reserves p.310, battle set-up p.306).

## Files

- `Extra-missions_merged_removed.pdf` - all 20 missions with fluff, deployment maps, primary/secondary objectives, and per-mission special rules.
