# Echoes of Heresy Event Pack - Detachments as Rites of War

By Robert Jardine

A set of five additional detachments used in place of Rites of War, originally written for the Echoes of Heresy event series.

## Contents

- Category: detachment
- Detachments: 5 (Drop Pod Assault, Fury of the Ancients, Pride of the Legion, Sky-Hunter Phalanx, Armoured Spearhead)
- Play style: narrative
- Points range: any

## Compatibility

- Replaces: rites of war
- Requires: nothing
- Do not use alongside another set that replaces rites of war.

## Files

- `The_Valley_of_Fallen_Sons_Event_Pack_Pg_6_7.pdf` — the five detachments with their slot restrictions and Prime Advantages, plus event contact details.
