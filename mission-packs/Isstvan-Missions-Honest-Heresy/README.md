# Istvaan Missions

By Honest Heresy

A five-mission narrative campaign following a force cut off behind enemy lines: clashing to gain a foothold, re-establishing comms, securing supply drops, holding the line, and evacuating the high command. Includes two new secondaries (Supply Drop and Domination), rules changes for Line and Vanguard scoring, terrain type definitions, kill-point victory rules, and five per-player award conditions.

- Category: mission pack
- Missions: 5
- Play style: narrative campaign
