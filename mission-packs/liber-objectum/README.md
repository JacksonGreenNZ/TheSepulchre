# Liber Objectum

By Sean 'Seanron' McCarter

An unofficial mission pack for Warhammer: The Horus Heresy - Age of Darkness (3rd Edition). Collates and updates fan-favourite deployment maps and missions from Forge World, Games Workshop, Goonhammer, and The Panoptica Team into 3rd Edition, integrating the Line, Vanguard, and Expendable rules. Contents split into alternative deployment maps, expansion missions, and legendary missions.

## Contents

- Category: mission pack
- Deployment maps: 11 (Search & Destroy, Hammer & Anvil, Dawn of War, Clash of the Line, Ambush, Vanguard Strike, Stronghold, Heavy Ordnance, Valley of Death, Arrowhead, Gauntlet)
- Expansion missions: 11 (Blood Feud, Onslaught, Shatter Strike, Tide of Carnage, War of Lies, Push the Line, Hold All Quarters, Bitter Endurance, Scything Blow, Relics of Conquest, Anvil Strike)
- Legendary missions: 3 (The Invasion of Lowen VII, Minefields of Mazandaran, Weapons of the Dark Age)
- Play style: matched/narrative, general-purpose
- Points range: any

## Compatibility

- Replaces: nothing
- Requires: nothing
- Additive. Provides an alternative Step 5 deployment-map selection and extra missions layered on the core three; use the core method or these by agreement. Requires the Age of Darkness 3rd Edition Core Rulebook and a faction Liber/PDF to play. Author notes future GW expansions supersede this unless both players agree otherwise.

## Files

- `Liber_Objectum_v5.pdf` - foreword, deployment maps (pp.4-9), expansion missions (pp.10-22), and legendary missions, with maps, victory conditions, and per-mission special rules.
