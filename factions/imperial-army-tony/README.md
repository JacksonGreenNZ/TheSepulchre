# The Imperial Army - The Anvil of the Emperor

By Tony Formosa

A fan-made supplement for The Horus Heresy: Age of Darkness, covering the regular Imperial Army (Excertus Imperialis), the professional mortal line regiments that formed the backbone of the Emperor's war machine. It introduces a full faction with its own army list, a Doctrines system for army-wide customisation, and an Orders system of actions and reactions that sets the Imperial Army apart on the battlefield.

## Contents

- Category: faction (complete army list)
- Doctrines: army-wide upgrades across four categories (Alternate Structures, Restricted Units, Skills and Drills, Equipment)
- Orders: Imperial Army actions and reactions (e.g. First Rank Fire Second Rank Fire, Take Cover Go to Ground, Bring it Down, Fire on my Target)
- Detachments: 6 (Infantry Platoon, Drop Troop Platoon, Morale Officer Section, Sentinel Patrol, Hardened Veterans, Rough Rider Phalanx)
- Units: full roster spanning High Command, Command, Troops, Transport, Support, Heavy Assault, Elites, Recon, Fast Attack, Armour, Heavy Transport, War Engines and Lord of War
- Special Characters: 6 (Ireton Masade, Saul Niborran, Xanda Javian, Somori Ture, The Operative)
- Armoury: full ranged and melee weapon profiles plus special rules and wargear
- Play style: combined-arms, order-driven infantry and armour
- Points range: any

## Compatibility

- Replaces: nothing
- Requires: nothing
- Self-contained. Uses the core Age of Darkness rulebook. Restricted Imperialis Militia units may be unlocked via Doctrine points as described in the list; allied Solar Auxilia and Mechanicum options reference their respective Libers for cost only.
