# Vital Target

By Jackson Green

An additional scoring method for Warhammer: The Horus Heresy (Third Edition). Each player builds one 100mm-based hobby centrepiece — a Target — that represents their army. Holding your own intact Target scores points; destroying the enemy's is worth double. Includes a full terrain profile, deployment and destruction rules, Victory Point values, clarifications, optional rules, and a per-Legion thematic inspiration table.

## Contents

- Category: other (scoring method)
- Scoring methods: 1
- Play style: narrative, hobby-focused; usable in any Age of Darkness battle by mutual agreement
- Points range: any (optional scaling above 3,000)

## Compatibility

- Replaces: nothing
- Requires: nothing
- Self-contained. Adds an optional scoring method on top of any mission; use by mutual agreement.
